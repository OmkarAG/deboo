ABOUT Us



Home is where you trust the toilet seat, and the same should apply to every public restroom. That's why we've made it our mission to provide you with a clean, hygienic, and safe experience every time you need to use a public toilet. At a deboo, your toilet seat gets cleaned and dried after (before) every use. It is our view that improved sanitation is more than just a service that provides comfort. Good sanitation promotes human dignity and empowers individuals and communities to reach their full potential. Our commitment to cleanliness is unwavering, we go above and beyond to ensure a clean and welcoming restroom for you. Join us in creating a world where good sanitation is not just a necessity but a fundamental human right.
