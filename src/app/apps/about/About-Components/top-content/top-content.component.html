Cleanliness Guaranteed.Every Time!

deboo™ offers intelligent public toilets that guarantee a clean and sanitised facility for every user every time they wish to use it.

Protecting People, Enhancing Lives

At deboo™ we believe that clean and safe public toilets are not a luxury but an essential part of the community. With the fusion of innovation and ergonomic design, deboo™ aims to revolutionise the perception of public toilets.

Self Cleaning Toilet Seat

deboo™ assures safe and hygienic sanitation using smart technology. A deboo™ toilet seat is cleaned and sanitised after every use so that every user gets a freshly cleaned seat surface. We have considered minute details to offer the best hygiene and user experience. (Patent Pending)

Intelligent Toilet

Powered by the Internet of Things (IoT), each deboo™ is equipped with a plethora of sensors and activators to reduce reliance on humans to maintain the cleanliness of the facility.

Touchless

To minimise the spread of infections a deboo™ facility is equipped with touchless sanitiser dispensers, soap dispensers, taps, flushes, bins, switches and door locking and unlocking systems.

Contactless

deboo™ offers cashless payment for entry to the facility and vending of sanitary pads. deboo™ app is available to download from Play Store and App Store

Environmentally Friendly

Our auto-flushing toilets optimise the usage of water by identifying whether solids or liquids are to be flushed. The use of electricity for lighting, ventilation and running of the facility is optimised based on the presence of a user.

Attended Toilet

Each facility is served by round the clock deboo™ Hostesses to assure safety and cleanliness.

Be Clean, Feel Fresh

Change the pads in privacy, safety and hygiene of deboo™. An automatic sanitary pad vending machine is located inside the deboo™ stall. Make a payment for vending the pad digitally using the deboo™ app.

Baby Diaper Change

Change your little ones in the safety and hygiene of a diaper changing station provided inside a deboo™ stall.

Wheelchair Access

deboo™ facility features a raised toilet bowl, grab rail, space to the side and front of the bowl to make it easier for our wheelchair using patrons.

Made In India

deboo™ has been conceptualised, designed & built in India. We have aimed to offer elegant design, smart performance and a premium product so that our patrons can experience luxury and comfort alongside hygiene.

Swachh Bharat Abhiyan

deboo™ is proud to contribute to India’s sanitation infrastructure and support the Clean India Mission.
